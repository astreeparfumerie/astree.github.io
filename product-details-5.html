Villa De La Rose.

"A Day at the Park" the essence of a serene, sunny afternoon spent in a lush, green park. The scent is a harmonious blend of natural and comforting notes, evoking a sense of tranquility and simple pleasures.

The scent is sweet and powdery, with a calming effect that makes you feel like you're lying on soft grass, with the warmth of the sun on your skin. The blend of fig, poppy, barley, and tonka beans creates a balanced fragrance that is both refreshing and comforting, ideal for those seeking a gentle and serene olfactory experience.

Black Pepper, Magnolia

Middle:

May Rose, Damask Rose, Geranium

Base:

Patchouli, Rose, Moss, Amberwood
